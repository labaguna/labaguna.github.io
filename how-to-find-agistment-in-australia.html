How to find agistment in Australia

By Johan
March 31, 2016

There are several agistment directory in Australia, but most are not uptodate and they charge high listing fees. We aim to change that by making an agistment directory that offers free listing. DailyGraze.com.au empowers livestock owners and landowners to create and maintain their own listings. We hope that this service will help farmers seeking agistment and offering agistment to easily find one another.
