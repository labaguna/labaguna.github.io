Why I am not a postmodernist

By Johan
October 11, 2018

One of the fundamental tenets of postmodernism is that there is no absolute truth. Now if that is true, then it is an absolute truth. But that means there is absolute truth, and therefor the tenet is false.

When you deconstruct the foundation of where you stand on, eventually it comes crumbling down along with yourself.

Johan
